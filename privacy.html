Privacy Policy - FindMyRide

Effective date: 2026-03-05

This Privacy Policy explains how FindMyRide (“the App”) handles information. The App is provided by Jonathan Sng (“we”, “us”).

Summary

  • Parking details (level, lot, zone, lift lobby, photo) are stored locally on your device.
  • If you grant location permission, the App saves GPS coordinates locally so you can open walking directions.
  • The App may show ads using Google AdMob. Ad providers may collect device identifiers and related data to serve ads.

Information the App uses

1) Information you enter

Level, Lot Number, Zone/Color, Lift Lobby, and an optional photo are saved to your device storage so you can retrieve your parking location later.

2) Location data (optional)

If you allow location permission, the App obtains your current location when you save a parking spot and stores the coordinates locally on your device. This is used to generate a “Directions to Vehicle” link.

3) Camera (optional)

If you use the photo feature, the App uses your device camera to capture an image. The photo is stored locally on your device.

4) Notifications (optional)

If you allow notifications, the App may schedule a reminder notification about your saved parking spot.

5) Advertising (AdMob)

If ads are enabled, the App uses Google AdMob to display banner ads. AdMob and its partners may collect and use information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, ad interactions, and diagnostics to provide and measure ads. This data is handled by Google under Google’s policies.

How information is shared

  • We do not operate a backend server for this App, and we do not receive your saved parking details.
  • Advertising providers (for example, Google AdMob) may receive device and ad-related information to serve ads.

Data retention

Your saved parking information remains on your device until you clear it within the App or uninstall the App.

Your choices

  • You can deny Location, Camera, or Notifications permissions. The App will still work with reduced features.
  • You can clear saved parking info inside the App.

Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above.
